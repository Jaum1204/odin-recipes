Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Dry lasagna noodles
  • Grounf beef
  • Marinara sauce
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish
  2. Start with a layer of sauce
  3. Add a layer of pasta sheets
  4. Add ground beef
  5. Add sauce and cheese
  6. Repeat the layers
  7. Top with sauce and cheese
